FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

01 IS EQUIPMENT PROVIDED FOR THE LESSON? ARE BALLS INCLUDED IN THE LESSON?
Yes, if you don’t have your own clubs we can provide all the equipment needed. However, balls for the lesson will have to be purchased separately.
02WHERE ARE LESSONS TAKEN?
Lessons will be undertaken in designated private teaching areas. Short game lessons are also available and will take place in the short game area and includes lessons on putting, chipping, pitching and bunker play.
03WILL TECHNOLOGY BE USED IN EACH LESSON?
Yes, a mix of 3D flightscope and 2D video work will be used depending on your requirements.
